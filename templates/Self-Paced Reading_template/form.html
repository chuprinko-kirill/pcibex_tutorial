In this experiment you will be asked to “read” some sentences. They will be masked under the "__" symbols at first. After you press the spacebar key the first word becomes unmasked and you can read it. When you press it again, the read word becomes masked and the new one appears. The process repeats untill you finish reading the sentence.
After some sentences you have to answer comprehension questions to show that you paid attention to what you just have read.
